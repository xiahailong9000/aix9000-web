Mobile app applications respect and protect users' privacy

Mobile app applications will be a highly diligent and prudent obligation to treat privacy information. Without your permission, mobile app application information will not be disclosed or provided to third parties

1. Scope

When you register an account, you provide a personal mobile app application based on the registration information requirements

2. Information usage

The purpose of mobile APP applications for serving users is to only display user information to friends allowed by the user

3. Information Disclosure

In the following situations, mobile app applications will disclose all or part of your personal information in accordance with legal provisions or your personal wishes:
a) With your consent, disclose to a third party B) Provide you with the requested products and services, and must share your personal information with third parties C) In accordance with relevant provisions of laws, administrative or judicial requests, disclose to third parties or administrative or judicial authorities If you violate relevant laws, regulations, rules, or service agreements, we need to disclose them to third parties If you are a qualified intellectual property and the complainant submits a complaint, the complainant should request disclosure of the complaint so that both parties can resolve the rights dispute F) Disclose as deemed appropriate by other mobile app applications, regulations, or policy websites in accordance with the law

4. Information security

a) The mobile app account has security features, please keep your username and password information. Mobile APP applications use security measures such as user password encryption to ensure that your information is not lost, abused, or altered. Despite the aforementioned security measures, please note that there is no "absolutely secure" information network

b) When using online trading, it is inevitable that the counterparty or potential counterparty will disclose their personal information, such as contact information and mailing address. Please protect your personal information and provide it to others only when necessary
